Pillar · About

One hundred years of helping neighbors step out of homelessness.

The Road Home is a private nonprofit social services agency that assists individuals and families experiencing homelessness in Salt Lake County.

Mission

The mission of The Road Home is to help people step out of homelessness and back into our community.

The Road Home is a private nonprofit social services agency that has assisted individuals and families experiencing homelessness in Salt Lake County since 1923. We provide emergency shelter, supportive services, and housing assistance every night of the year.

What we believe

Core values

Seven commitments that shape how we show up — with guests, with each other, with donors, and with partners.

01

Equal treatment

We are committed to providing equal treatment to all who come to us in need.

02

Compassion

We will nurture our love for humanity within each of our relationships and interactions.

03

Respect

Respect will be the cornerstone of our conduct.

04

Stewardship

We steward every dollar, volunteer hour, and partnership as if the next resident depended on it.

05

Integrity

We say what we do and we do what we say — with donors, guests, partners, and staff alike.

06

Community

Homelessness ends when neighbors show up for neighbors. We are not a charity; we are a community response.
