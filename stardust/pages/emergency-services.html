Get Help · Emergency Services

A human on the phone. A bed tonight. Help now.

When hours matter, The Road Home's emergency services team is the fastest way to connect with shelter, outreach, and crisis support in Salt Lake County.

801-990-9999Homeless Services Line · 24 hours

The front of every other program.

Emergency services sit at the front of every other program we run. Whether someone is calling for the first time, being referred by a hospital discharge planner, or found outside in a freezing night, the emergency-services team is the first voice they hear.

We meet people where they are — on the phone, on the street, at the hospital bedside, or walking through the resource-center doors.

No intake paperwork is required to start. No ID is required. What we need first is a conversation.

Three ways we respond

What emergency services covers.

One phone number, three response modes. The team dispatches based on what's needed and where the person is.

01

Phone intake

24-hour Homeless Services Line. Interpreters available. First triage, bed availability, family services, partner referrals.

02

Street outreach

Teams that visit encampments and unsheltered individuals to connect them with shelter, medical care, and case management.

03

Weather response

Expanded capacity during extreme weather. Emergency hotel placement for the medically vulnerable.

Extreme weather

If the weather turns dangerous, so do we.

Below freezing or above one hundred degrees, we expand shelter capacity and activate outreach. If you see someone outside in dangerous weather, call 801-990-9999 — or 911 if it's a medical emergency.

Activation thresholds

≤ 32°
Cold-weather protocol activates: expanded capacity, outreach doubles, hotel placement for medically vulnerable.
≥ 100°
Heat-wave protocol: cooling stations at resource centers, water-distribution outreach, hotel placement on request.
Flash
Storm / flood / air-quality events: coordinated response with Salt Lake County emergency management.

For partners

Making a referral?

Hospitals, jails, law enforcement, and partner nonprofits can make referrals directly. We respond within one hour during business hours; within four hours overnight.

Partner portal available at partners.theroadhome.org for secure, pre-filled referrals.
