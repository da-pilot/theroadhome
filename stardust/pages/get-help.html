The Path

How does it help?

Three programs, one path out. Each program meets someone at the moment of their greatest need — and stays with them through the one after that.

01 · Begin

Emergency Shelter

A safe bed, a warm meal, and a team trained to help. The first step for anyone without a safe place to sleep tonight.

Emergency services →
02 · Stabilize

Supportive Services

Case management, mental-health support, employment help, and benefits navigation. The work that stabilizes a life once the crisis has passed.

See services →
03 · Go home

Housing Assistance

Rapid rehousing, permanent supportive housing, rental assistance. The fastest way out of homelessness is a home.
