Why monthly matters

01

Predictable help

Recurring gifts let us plan housing placements months in advance. Stability at the organization becomes stability for the resident.

02

Lower overhead

Monthly donations cost less to process — more of each dollar reaches services. Less on bank fees, more on beds.

03

Community

Housing Champions receive quarterly program updates from our CEO and invitations to Palmer Court tours. You see the work up close.

A story of impact

Amanda found refuge.

Housing Champions make it possible for us to provide assistance and support from the moment an individual or family seeks shelter to the moment they move into a home of their own.

When you become a Housing Champion you'll be helping people like Amanda change their lives and create connections to the community that will last a lifetime.

Amanda had fled an abusive relationship with her children. With nowhere else to turn, Amanda found refuge at the Midvale Family Resource Center. Through case management and rental assistance funded by Housing Champions, Amanda and her children moved into permanent housing within four months.
