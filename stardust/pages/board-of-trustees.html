About · Governance

The 2026–2027 Board of Trustees.

Our board members are volunteers who help guide the programs and practices of The Road Home. They are a vital link to our community and provide oversight and support for the organization.

The Road Home is proudly ranked as a Four Star Charity by Charity Navigator for our organization's transparency and efficiency — a rating our board takes seriously. All board materials, minutes, and financials are available on request.
