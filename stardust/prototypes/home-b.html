What we do

Three programs, one path out. Each program meets someone at the moment of their greatest need — and stays with them through the one after that.

A safe bed, a warm meal, and a team trained to help. Resource centers serve individuals, couples, and families every night of the year.

A story · Palmer Court

The hardest thing about being unhoused wasn't sleeping outside. It was the paperwork. The Road Home sat with me through every form.
Darnell H. Permanent supportive housing 4 yr Read full story →

Darnell moved into Palmer Court in 2022 after eight months in and out of emergency shelter. His caseworker, Theresa, helped him replace his ID, enroll in Medicaid, and find part-time work at a hardware store in Sugar House.

Today he's working toward a CDL. He wants to drive for a delivery company. Read more stories →
