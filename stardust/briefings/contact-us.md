# Intent

Non-emergency contact form + every way to reach The Road Home. Clearly distinguished from the emergency / shelter-intake line.

# Audience

Donor with a question. Partner agency scheduling a meeting. Journalist. Neighbor with a concern.

# Sections

1. Global nav + breadcrumbs
2. Hero-light — "Get in touch"
3. Two-column layout: context sidebar (phones/emails/addresses) on the left, contact form on the right
4. Crisis callout — "Need shelter tonight? Call 801-990-9999" in a prominent band (so crisis audience is never routed into a non-emergency form)
5. Office hours + mailing address
6. Footer
