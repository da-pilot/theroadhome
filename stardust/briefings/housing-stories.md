# Intent

The human-first evidence page. A magazine-style editorial grid of client stories — "stories from the housing."

# Audience

**Donor** (primary) — wants to see the impact humanized.
**Policy / press** — wants named clients they can cite.
**Prospective resident** — rare but meaningful — wants to see people like themselves.

# Sections

1. Global nav + breadcrumbs
2. Hero — "Housing Stories"
3. Featured story (big card — Alex and Taylor or Randall)
4. Stories grid (6–12 cards, editorial)
5. BY THE NUMBERS band — verbatim data from live site
6. Share your story CTA (link to /tell-your-story/)
7. Footer
