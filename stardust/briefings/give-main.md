# Intent

Pillar hub for every donor pathway. Lead with a single strong "Give" CTA; route the reader to the right giving method one click away.

# Audience

**Donor** (primary) — wants to know their money works and where to click.
**Planned-giving prospect** (secondary) — evaluating legacy / major-gift options.

# Sections

1. Global nav
2. Pillar hero — "Your gift works here."
3. Triage band adapted: three primary giving paths (one-time / monthly / item)
4. Ways to give grid — 6 cards: one-time, Housing Champions (monthly), Legacy/Planned, Real Property, Stock/Wire Transfer, In-Kind, Employer Matching
5. Impact strip — where dollars go (adapted from `/where-does-it-go/`)
6. Phone/check/email footnote (the current site's $10-minimum + PayPal + check disclaimer, collapsed)
7. Footer
