# Intent

Primary donation conversion page. Minimal friction. One visible form with tabs for one-time / monthly, amount picker, impact math, and a secure-checkout footer.

# Audience

Donor who clicked DONATE in the nav. State: ready to give, wants it done in under 60 seconds.

# Sections

1. Global nav
2. Hero-light — single headline + one sentence of context + $-math strip above the fold
3. Amount picker card (one-time / monthly tabs; $25, $50, $100, $250, Custom)
4. Impact math band — "$85 = a night of shelter"
5. Testimonial — one short Darnell-style pull-quote
6. Security + tax-deductible cues + 4★ badge
7. Other ways to give (link band to /housing-champions/, item donations, legacy)
8. Footnote (verbatim disclaimers)
9. Footer
