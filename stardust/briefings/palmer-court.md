## At-a-glance sidebar
- **Units:** 201
- **Model:** Permanent supportive housing (Housing First)
- **Opened:** 2009
- **Location:** Salt Lake City, UT
- **Who we serve:** Adults and families who previously experienced long-term homelessness

## Narrative (synthesized, on-brand)
Palmer Court is proof that housing is the solution to homelessness. Every resident has their own apartment and access to on-site case management, behavioral-health support, and employment assistance. No time limit. No "graduation" requirement.

The building is not a shelter. It is a home.

## Who lives there
Residents are individuals and families who have experienced chronic homelessness — often combined with disability, serious mental illness, or substance use. Palmer Court meets them where they are.

### Ongoing weekly activities (verbatim)
- **Literacy Night — Kids' Book Club** · Volunteer to help spark interest and improve reading skills with children ages 5–12. Weekly, one-hour commitment. Must be 16 years or older.
- All volunteers for weekly activities must complete a volunteer orientation and background check. Three-month minimum commitment. Email volunteer@theroadhome.org for the schedule.

### One-time group activities (verbatim)
- **Donation Drive** — There is an ongoing need for donated items at Palmer Court, including apartment cleaning kits and move-in kits for families and individuals. Needs include bedding, towels, non-perishable food, books and movies.
- **Holidays** — Many ways for groups to get involved during the holiday season; collecting donations or planning holiday activities.
- **Be Creative** — Design your own project. We are flexible and try to accommodate ideas.

All volunteer group leaders must go over a Boundaries and Confidentiality form with their group and return it signed. Schedule your activity with one of our Volunteer Coordinators at volunteer@theroadhome.org or **385-351-2389**.
