## Programs grid (verbatim titles)
1. **Housing Navigation Services** — Guests at our facilities have access to general housing support and specialized housing navigation.
2. **Rapid Rehousing** — Short-term rental assistance + case management to help households move from shelter to stable housing quickly.
3. **Permanent Supportive Housing** — Long-term subsidized housing with wrap-around support (Palmer Court is the flagship).
4. **SSVF — Supportive Services for Veteran Families** — rapid rehousing + case management for veteran households.

## Housing navigation covers (verbatim list from live site)
- **Housing History Review** — We help guests gather and review their housing history to support applications and eligibility.
- **Personalized Housing Search** — We work with each household to understand what kind of housing they're looking for and what fits their budget.
