## What we do (three-beat)
- **Phone intake** — 24-hour Homeless Services Line. Interpreters available.
- **Street outreach** — teams that visit encampments and unsheltered individuals to connect them with shelter and medical care.
- **Warming & cooling support** — expanded capacity during extreme weather; emergency hotel placement for medically vulnerable.

## Warming / cooling protocol
When temperatures fall below [threshold] or rise above [threshold], we expand shelter capacity and activate outreach. If you see someone outside in dangerous weather, call **801-990-9999**.

## Partner referrals (synthesized)
Hospitals, jails, law enforcement, and partner nonprofits can make referrals directly. We respond within one hour during business hours; within four hours overnight.
