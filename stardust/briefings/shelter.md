## Narrative (verbatim)
Our Emergency Shelters, the South Salt Lake Men's Resource Center and the Midvale Family Resource Center, are open 365 days a year and serve people experiencing homelessness in Salt Lake County.

Along with providing life-saving emergency shelter, we help people re-build their support networks and create stability through supportive services and housing placement.

If you are in need of shelter please contact Utah Community Action's Homeless Services Line at 801-990-9999.
